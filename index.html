The birthplace of Gautam Bhuddha

In 623 BC, a baby destined to change the world was born in Lumbini, present-day Nepal. Queen Mayadevi, on her way home,gave birth under a sal tree, surprised by the early arrival. The full moon illuminated this momentous event,marking thebeginning of Buddhism in a place now known as Lumbini.
